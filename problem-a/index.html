Remy

This is Remy. He is the five-star underground chef in Paris in the movie Ratatouille.
He is a rat that can read, cook, and control a human by his hair, cooking the finest meal that surprises the food critiques of Paris.

I admire Remy for the following reasons:

  • He experimented cooking with lightnings and chimney smokes.
  • He served his colony by detecting rat poisons thanks to supreme smelling abilities.
  • He turned a garbage boy into the most famous chef in Paris.
